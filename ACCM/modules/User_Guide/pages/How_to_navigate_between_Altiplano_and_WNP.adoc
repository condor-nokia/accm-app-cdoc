= How to navigate between {main-base-app} and {app-short-name} Apps

== Introduction

This section provides the steps required to return to {main-base-app} from {app-short-name} and vice versa.

== Access to {main-base-app} from {app-short-name} WebUI

To return to {main-base-app} from {app-short-name} WebUI, execute the following steps:

. Two options can be used:
.. Click over the *Nokia* icon on the menu bar displayed on left top of the screen.
+
image::howReturnAltiplanoNokiaIcon_option1.png[align="left"]

.. Click over the *Apps* icon on the menu bar displayed on right top of the screen.
+
image::howReturnAltiplanoNokiaIcon_option2.png[align="left"]

. Will be redirected to {main-base-app} Access Controller Home page.
+
image::altiplanoHome.png[align="left"]

== Access to {app-short-name} from {main-base-app} WebUI

To return to {app-short-name} from {main-base-app} WebUI, execute the following steps:

. The {main-base-app} home App.
+
image::altiplanoHome.png[align="left"]

. Two options can be used:
.. All available Apps are displayed on the screen. Click over the *{app-full-name}* icon.
+
image::WNP_option1.png[align="left"]

.. Click over the on the *Application menu* displayed on right top of the screen.
+
image::altiplanoAppsMenu.png[align="left"]

... Then, select *{app-full-name}* menu option.
+
image::altiplanoAppsMenuAll.png[align="left"]

. Will be redirected to {app-short-name} Home page.
+
image::wnpHome.png[align="left"]
